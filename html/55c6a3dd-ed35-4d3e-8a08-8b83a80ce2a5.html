What is Habitual.AI?

How to use this demo...

Habitual.AI is the world’s first AI engine that automates the process of building habits. This patent-pending technology enables banks and financial institutions to boost habitual usage of their digital and mobile banking platforms. It learns how customers use a digital banking product and provides personalized feature recommendations that put them on the path to long-term loyalty and habitual usage.  By building customer habits, Habitual.AI has delivered a 145% increase in transactions, 45% reduction in customer churn and 75% increase in users who made 4 or more transactions a month.   This interactive demo is designed to simulate the feature recommendations that Habitual.AI is capable of generating for your unique customer base.  

- Enter the customer profile. - Select the features that are currently used by the customer. We suggest choosing one feature to get the most out of this demo. - Generate the Habitual.AI recommendation for your customer. - Control the user response to see how Habitual.AI dynamically responds to user behaviour:

> If the customer follows the recommendation, choose ‘Which feature should we recommend next?’ > If the customer ignores the recommendation, update the behavior parameter and generate a fresh recommendation.

The Habit Formation Path will track your customers progress throughout the demo interaction.
